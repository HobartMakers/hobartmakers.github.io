Some small print

You may become a social member regardless of how much you pay, but to become an ordinary member, you will need to pay a minimum of $10/month or $120/year.

All members (social and ordinary) can take part in all activities, with the following exception: social members may not a) vote at general meetings, or b) join the management committee.

Social members must also pay $5 extra towards each paid workshop that they attend, to help cover our operating costs.

Thank you!
